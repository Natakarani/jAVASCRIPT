operators

operators are used to perform some operations between the two operands

        1. arithematic operator=> addition+, substraction -,
        multiplication *, modulus%, increment++, decrement--, division /

        2. comparision opertor(relational operator)=> is equal to==, identical ===(compares value and data type also),
        not equal !=, not identical !==, greater than>, less than<, less than or equal to /,=, greater than equals to >=

        3.bitwise opertor=> bitwise AND &. bitwise OR |, bitwise not ~ ,bitwise left shift<<, bitwise right shift>>, bitwise right shift with zero >>>, bit wise left shift with zero>>>

        4. logical opertor=> logical AND && , logical OR || logical NOT ! 

        5.assignment opertor=> assign=, add and assign+=, sub and asign-=, mul and assign *=, division and assign/=, modulus and assign %=
         6. special opertor or ternary operator=> condition?"true":"false";
